# Growth Operations & Metrics

## Metrics That Matter

### By Channel

**Free Tools:**
- Organic traffic per tool.
- Conversion rate to main product.
- Cost per tool (time + hosting).

**Reddit/FB:**
- Comments and upvotes per post.
- Click-through rate to product.
- Signups from community traffic.

**Twitter/X:**
- Follower growth rate.
- Engagement rate (likes + replies + retweets / impressions).
- Profile click-through rate.
- Conversion from profile to signup.

**YouTube:**
- Views per video.
- Click-through rate from video to product.
- Cost per acquisition (if paid partnership).
- Long-term SEO traffic.

**TikTok:**
- Views per video.
- Profile visits.
- Click-through rate.
- Cost per click (paid ads).
- LTV vs. CAC.

### The North Star Metric

**For pre-launch:** Waitlist signups.
**For post-launch:** Weekly active users.
**For growth:** Revenue per channel.
**For scale:** LTV / CAC ratio (target: > 3).

## Distribution Decision Matrix

| If your users are... | Use... |
|------|------|
| Developers, tech-savvy | GitHub, Hacker News, Twitter, Reddit |
| Designers, creatives | Dribbble, Behance, Instagram, Pinterest |
| Business professionals | LinkedIn, Twitter, newsletters |
| Young consumers | TikTok, Instagram, YouTube |
| Niche hobbyists | Reddit, Facebook Groups, Discord |
| Enterprise buyers | LinkedIn, cold email, webinars |

## AI-Assisted Distribution

### Content Generation at Scale
- Use AI to generate 100+ ad creatives.
- Use AI to write social media posts in your voice.
- Use AI to create video scripts from blog posts.

### Automated Posting
- Schedule posts across platforms using Buffer, Hootsuite, or Make.com.
- Auto-repost top-performing content.
- Cross-post from one channel to others.

### Community Monitoring
- Use AI to monitor Reddit, Twitter, and forums for mentions of your keywords.
- Auto-generate helpful replies.
- Alert you when a high-value conversation is happening.

### Analytics & Optimization
- AI analyzes which creatives perform best.
- Auto-pause underperforming ads.
- Auto-scale winning campaigns.
- Generate weekly performance reports.
