# Marketing Psychology & Mental Models

## Yappi.ai Context

We serve small business owners who are often overwhelmed. Our goal is to reduce cognitive load and demonstrate immediate value.

### Key Models for Yappi.ai

- **Jobs to Be Done**: Users "hire" Yappi to "insure their revenue" by never missing a call.
- **The "Wow" Moment**: The "Call Me Now" feature in onboarding provides immediate proof of value (Reciprocity & Mere Exposure).
- **Reducing Friction**: Zero-friction onboarding by scraping business data (IKEA Effect - they see their own data pre-filled).

## Influencing Behavior

- **Social Proof**: Use reviews and testimonials in the `ReviewsWall` component.
- **Scarcity/Urgency**: Highlight the "7-Day Missed Call Challenge" for trial users.
- **Loss Aversion**: Frame missed calls as "lost revenue" that Yappi prevents.
- **Anchoring**: Use pricing tiers to anchor the value of the "Defender" plan.

## Implementation

- **Onboarding**: Keep steps minimal to avoid the Paradox of Choice.
- **Dashboard**: Use the Zeigarnik Effect by showing "Pending Actions" or "Missed Leads" to encourage engagement.
