# Creator Partnerships & Paid Growth

## Channel 5: YouTube Creator Partnerships

### The Two Models

**Model 1: Promoters**
- Pay creators cash or affiliate revenue.
- Best for: Proven products with existing traction.
- Risk: One-time promotion. No long-term commitment.

**Model 2: Co-Founders**
- Give creators equity.
- Best for: Early-stage products needing distribution.
- Advantage: Creator is invested in long-term success.

### Why YouTube Over Other Platforms

1. **Best AI-positive investment.** AI tools are inherently visual and demonstrable. YouTube shows the product in action.
2. **Predictable outcomes.** A creator with 100K subscribers can reliably drive 1K+ signups.
3. **Long tail.** YouTube videos rank in search and drive traffic for years.
4. **Trust transfer.** Viewers trust the creator. Their recommendation is an endorsement.

### How to Partner with Creators

1. **Identify creators in your niche.** Use tools like Social Blade or manual research.
2. **Engage authentically.** Comment on their videos. Share their content. Build a relationship.
3. **Pitch with numbers.** "I built [Product]. It solves [Problem]. Your audience has this problem. I think we could do $X MRR together."
4. **Offer equity or revenue share.** Creators are businesses too. They care about ROI.
5. **Give them creative control.** They know their audience. Let them market your product their way.

### Scaling Creator Partnerships

- **Start with one.** Get one successful partnership.
- **Use success to attract more.** "We worked with [Creator] and hit $10K MRR. Want to do the same?"
- **Expand to other platforms.** YouTube success opens doors to TikTok, Instagram, and newsletters.
- **Build a creator program.** Formalize partnerships with standardized deals and affiliate tracking.

## Channel 6: TikTok (Organic + Paid)

### The Marketing-First Approach

For mobile apps, TikTok is often the primary distribution channel.

### The Viral Content Framework

**Hook (0-3 seconds):**
- Capture attention immediately.
- Use sound, motion, surprise, or curiosity.
- Examples: Shaking a glass bowl, a shocking statistic, a bold claim.

**Problem (3-10 seconds):**
- Clearly state the problem or storyline.
- Make it relatable.
- Example: "Vapes are unhealthy. I wanted to quit but couldn't."

**Solution (10-20 seconds):**
- Show how your product solves it.
- Demonstrate the magic moment.
- Example: "So I built an app that pays people to ditch their vapes."

**Call to Action (Last 2 seconds):**
- Show the product clearly.
- Use text overlay: "Link in bio" or "Download now."

### The Organic-to-Paid Pipeline

**Step 1: Organic Validation**
- Post 10-20 videos.
- Track which ones get traction.
- Identify winners (high views, high engagement, low cost per click).

**Step 2: Paid Scaling**
- Take your top 3 organic videos.
- Upload them to TikTok Ads Manager.
- Set a small budget ($20-$50/day).
- Measure cost per click and cost per acquisition.

**Step 3: Optimize**
- Kill ads with CPC > your target.
- Double down on ads with CPC < your target.
- Test new variations of winning creatives.

**Step 4: Scale**
- Increase budget on winners.
- Expand to lookalike audiences.
- Test new ad formats (Spark Ads, Collection Ads).

### The Math

- **Target:** LTV > 3x CAC.
- **Example:** If LTV = $70 and CAC = $24, you have a profitable funnel.
- **Scaling signal:** When CPC is consistently under $0.50 with good conversion rates.

## Cross-Channel Amplification

### The Content Multiplier
One piece of content should become 10:

1. **Twitter thread** → Long-form blog post.
2. **Blog post** → YouTube video script.
3. **YouTube video** → Shorts/Reels/TikTok clips.
4. **Shorts** → LinkedIn carousel.
5. **LinkedIn post** → Newsletter email.
6. **Newsletter** → Podcast episode.
7. **Podcast** → Audiogram clips for social.
8. **All of the above** → Free tool landing page.

### The Distribution Calendar

| Day | Channel | Action |
|-----|---------|--------|
| Monday | Twitter | Educational thread |
| Tuesday | Reddit | Helpful comment in 3 communities |
| Wednesday | YouTube | Publish or pitch to creator |
| Thursday | TikTok | Post 2 organic videos |

| Sunday | Plan | Review analytics, plan next week |

## Creator Partnership Checklist

- [ ] Identified 10+ creators in my niche.
- [ ] Engaged authentically for 2+ weeks before pitching.
- [ ] Pitched with clear numbers (target MRR, affiliate %, equity %).
- [ ] Gave creative control to the creator.
- [ ] Validated TikTok content organically before paid ads.
- [ ] LTV > 3x CAC before scaling paid acquisition.
- [ ] Repurposing content across 3+ channels.
- [ ] Partnered with at least 1 creator for distribution.
