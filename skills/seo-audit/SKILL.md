# SEO Audit & Optimization

## Yappi.ai SEO Strategy

Our primary SEO surface area is the **Public Profile Pages** (`yappi.ai/p/[slug]`). These pages must rank for local business searches.

### Key Focus Areas

1. **Local SEO**: Ensure public profiles include business name, city, and services in metadata.
2. **Technical SEO**: Fast loading times (Core Web Vitals) and proper canonicalization.
3. **Content Quality**: Dynamic content from scraped reviews and business descriptions.

## Checklist

- **Title Tags**: `[Business Name] in [City] | Powered by Yappi.ai`
- **Meta Descriptions**: Clear value prop for the business and a call to action (e.g., "Book now").
- **Structured Data**: Use JSON-LD for `LocalBusiness` on public profiles.
- **Mobile-First**: Ensure the `biz/[slug]` pages are perfectly responsive.
