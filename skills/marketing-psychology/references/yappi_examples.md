# Yappi.ai Examples

Concrete illustrations of the decision model in `marketing-psychology` applied
to the Yappi.ai context (a missed-call revenue-insurance product for small
business owners). Pulled when the user is working on Yappi specifically and
wants worked examples.

## Yappi.ai Context

We serve small business owners who are often overwhelmed. Our goal is to
reduce cognitive load and demonstrate immediate value.

## Key Models mapped to Yappi

- **Jobs to Be Done**: Users "hire" Yappi to "insure their revenue" by never
  missing a call.
- **The "Wow" Moment**: The "Call Me Now" feature in onboarding provides
  immediate proof of value (Reciprocity & Mere Exposure).
- **Reducing Friction**: Zero-friction onboarding by scraping business data
  (IKEA Effect — they see their own data pre-filled).

## Influencing Behavior (Yappi surfaces)

- **Social Proof**: Use reviews and testimonials in the `ReviewsWall` component.
- **Scarcity/Urgency**: Highlight the "7-Day Missed Call Challenge" for trial
  users.
- **Loss Aversion**: Frame missed calls as "lost revenue" that Yappi prevents.
- **Anchoring**: Use pricing tiers to anchor the value of the "Defender" plan.

## Implementation (Yappi)

- **Onboarding**: Keep steps minimal to avoid the Paradox of Choice.
- **Dashboard**: Use the Zeigarnik Effect by showing "Pending Actions" or
  "Missed Leads" to encourage engagement.

## Mapping back to the decision model

| Yappi surface | Primary principle | Secondary principle |
|---|---|---|
| Onboarding flow | IKEA effect | Paradox of choice (avoid) |
| Pricing tier comparison | Anchoring | Loss aversion |
| `ReviewsWall` component | Social proof | Reciprocity |
| Trial "7-Day Challenge" nudge | Scarcity/urgency (real) | Commitment & consistency |
| Dashboard "Pending leads" | Zeigarnik effect | Loss aversion |
| "Call Me Now" onboarding step | Reciprocity | Fogg: high ability + prompt |
